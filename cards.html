Student Identity card (isic)

The ISIC card is your ultimate student lifestyle card. Want awesome travel deals, money off online shopping, discounts on restaurants, cafes and takeaways? We've got you covered, ISIC is the only internationally-recognized student ID. It is your ticket to fantastic discounts and services in Sri Lanka and globally.
There are over 150,000 discounts worldwide - so you will never be short of a bargain. Want to know more, Check out our local discounts and our international discounts.

am i eligible?

If you are a full-time student at school, college or university, aged 12 or over, you can apply fire student card with ISIC. It's a hassle-free process, and in no time at all you•11 be enjoying thou-sands of discounts and benefits at home and away.

If you are not a full time student, you may be eligible for our youth card (if you're 30 years old or younger) or our teacher card (if you are a full time teacher).
